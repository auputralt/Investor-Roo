        <h2>Project Background</h2>
        <p>
          This website presents a structured, decision-oriented report for agricultural investment opportunities in <strong>Lampung Utara</strong> and <strong>Maluku</strong> provinces of Indonesia. The strategy is oriented toward positioning for Kementerian Pertanian (Kementan) support and local government alignment.
        </p>
        
        <h3>Purpose</h3>
        <p>
          The information presented here serves multiple purposes:
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Internal Planning:</strong> Guide strategic decision-making for investment activities</li>
          <li><strong>Investor Communication:</strong> Present clear opportunities and risk assessments</li>
          <li><strong>Government Engagement:</strong> Support discussions with Kementan and local governments</li>
          <li><strong>Stakeholder Alignment:</strong> Provide a shared reference for all parties</li>
        </ul>
        
        <h3>Data Status</h3>
        <p>
          Throughout this website, data is labeled with its verification status:
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li><span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span> - Verified from reliable sources</li>
          <li><span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span> - Inferred from patterns or general knowledge</li>
          <li><span class="data-status uncertain">Uncertain</span> - Requires verification from local sources</li>
        </ul>
