    <h2>Proposal Strategy</h2>
    
    <div class="grid grid-2">
      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">
          <h3><i class="fas fa-bolt" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Quick-Win Demonstration</h3>
        </div>
        <div class="card-body">
          <h4>Maize + Upland Rice Intercropping</h4>
          <ul>
            <li>Design 1-2 demonstration clusters (1,000-3,000 ha equivalent)</li>
            <li>Link Kementan seed and Alsintan support to specific farmer groups</li>
            <li>Model similar to Maluku Tengah's 2025 package</li>
          </ul>
          
          <h4>Key Advantages</h4>
          <ul>
            <li>4-5 month ROI - fast results</li>
            <li>LER 2.11-2.45 proven productivity gain</li>
            <li>Compatible with existing Kementan programs</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-header">
          <h3><i class="fas fa-flask" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Bioeconomy Development</h3>
        </div>
        <div class="card-body">
          <h4>Spices & Coconut Value Addition</h4>
          <ul>
            <li>Multi-year proposals to DG Perkebunan for rejuvenation</li>
            <li>Companion proposal for mini-distillation plants (nutmeg/clove oil)</li>
            <li>Coconut processing units (VCO, coconut sugar)</li>
          </ul>
          
          <h4>Key Advantages</h4>
          <ul>
            <li>Significant value addition (e.g., nutmeg oil ~IDR 335,000/kg)</li>
            <li>Export-oriented high-value products</li>
            <li>Aligns with provincial bioeconomy narrative</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<!-- Application Process -->
<section class="section-sm section-cream">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>Application Process</h2>
    
    <div class="timeline">
      <div class="timeline-item">
        <div class="timeline-date">Step 1</div>
        <div class="timeline-title">Engage Traditional Leaders</div>
        <div class="timeline-content">
          <ul>
            <li>Engage adat (customary) leaders early in the process</li>
            <li>Secure written agreements with benefit-sharing for communities</li>
            <li>Address land tenure issues proactively</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div class="timeline-item">
        <div class="timeline-date">Step 2</div>
        <div class="timeline-title">Coordinate with Dinas Pertanian</div>
        <div class="timeline-content">
          <ul>
            <li>Build on Maluku Tengah's proven absorption capacity</li>
            <li>Submit proposals through established channels</li>
            <li>Leverage existing farmer group networks</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div class="timeline-item">
        <div class="timeline-date">Step 3</div>
        <div class="timeline-title">Develop Cluster-Based Value Chains</div>
        <div class="timeline-content">
          <ul>
            <li>Focus on individual islands for processing near production</li>
            <li>Prioritize high value-to-weight commodities (spices, oils)</li>
            <li>Build local processing capacity</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div class="timeline-item">
        <div class="timeline-date">Step 4</div>
        <div class="timeline-title">Multi-Agency Coordination</div>
        <div class="timeline-content">
          <ul>
            <li>Coordinate with Kemenperin for processing infrastructure</li>
            <li>Engage provincial government for bioeconomy narrative</li>
            <li>Secure endorsement from Governor and Bupati</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
