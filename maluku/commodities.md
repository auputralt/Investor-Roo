    <div id="nutmeg" class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header">
        <h2><i class="fas fa-seedling" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Nutmeg (Pala)</h2>
        <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed - Main Historical Commodity</span>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3>Overview</h3>
            <p>Nutmeg is one of Maluku's main historical and current commodities. Harvested area and production increased 2020-2024. <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></p>
            
            <h4>Agro-Ecological Fit</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Performs well in humid tropical conditions with well-drained soils</li>
              <li>Maluku has long agro-ecological and cultural fit</li>
              <li>Acidic soils in many parts are suitable for nutmeg</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Market Analysis</h4>
            <ul>
              <li><strong>Export:</strong> Strong demand for whole nutmeg and derivative products</li>
              <li><strong>Value Addition:</strong> Nutmeg oil can add up to ~IDR 335,000/kg vs raw nutmeg <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3>Financial Metrics</h3>
            <table class="mb-lg">
              <tr>
                <th>Yield Potential</th>
                <td>Several hundred kg/ha dried nutmeg in well-managed systems</td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Current Productivity</th>
                <td>Below potential due to input and management constraints <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>ROI Horizon</th>
                <td>5-7 years to full production <span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Rejuvenation Strategy</th>
                <td>Under-planting can shorten time to revenue</td>
              </tr>
            </table>
            
            <h4>Value Addition Opportunities</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Nutmeg oil (essential oil)</li>
              <li>Oleoresin extraction</li>
              <li>Processed food products</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Cloves -->
    <div id="cloves" class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header">
        <h2><i class="fas fa-spa" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Cloves (Cengkeh)</h2>
        <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed - Key Commodity</span>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3>Overview</h3>
            <p>Cloves are a key commodity in Maluku, similar to North Maluku where acidic soils support clove plantations. <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></p>
            
            <h4>Agro-Ecological Fit</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Demands humid climate with pronounced dry season</li>
              <li>Present in many Maluku islands</li>
              <li>Acidic soils support clove production</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Market Analysis</h4>
            <ul>
              <li><strong>Global Prices:</strong> High-value spice with prices up to ~USD 7,000/ton <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></li>
              <li><strong>Export:</strong> Strong structural demand in global spice trade</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3>Financial Metrics</h3>
            <table class="mb-lg">
              <tr>
                <th>Yield Potential</th>
                <td>0.5-1.0 t/ha dry cloves in well-managed orchards <span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Current Productivity</th>
                <td>Limited by aging trees and input constraints</td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>ROI Horizon</th>
                <td>4-7 years to strong yields <span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>NPV Potential</th>
                <td>Extremely attractive if markets are secured</td>
              </tr>
            </table>
            
            <h4>Value Addition Opportunities</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Clove oil (eugenol extraction)</li>
              <li>Pharmaceutical applications</li>
              <li>Food and beverage industry</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3>Overview</h3>
            <p>Coconut is a major staple commodity in Maluku with increasing production and harvested area 2020-2024. <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></p>
            
            <h4>Agro-Ecological Fit</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Suited to coastal zones and atoll/island environments</li>

              <li>Existing farmer expertise</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Market Analysis</h4>
            <ul>
              <li><strong>Domestic:</strong> Strong demand for fresh nuts, copra, cooking oil</li>
              <li><strong>Export:</strong> Higher-value derivatives (VCO, coconut sugar, activated carbon) <span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span></li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>

            <table class="mb-lg">

              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Replanting ROI</th>

    <!-- Sago -->
    <div id="sago" class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header">
        <h2><i class="fas fa-water" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Sago (Sagu)</h2>
        <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed - Key Staple & Bioeconomy Target</span>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3>Overview</h3>
            <p>Sago is one of Maluku's key staples and bioeconomy targets. Production and harvested area have grown 2020-2024. <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></p>
            
            <h4>Agro-Ecological Fit</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Thrives in swampy/wetland areas where other crops perform poorly</li>
              <li>Aligns with Maluku's swampy lowlands</li>
              <li>Indigenous carbohydrate source</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Market Analysis</h4>
            <ul>
              <li><strong>Local:</strong> Food security commodity</li>
              <li><strong>Industrial:</strong> Emerging uses for starch and bio-materials <span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span></li>
              <li><strong>Policy:</strong> Strong interest as indigenous carbohydrate source</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3>Financial Metrics</h3>
            <table class="mb-lg">
              <tr>
                <th>Yield Potential</th>
                <td>High starch yield per hectare, often exceeding rice in energy terms</td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Productivity</th>
                <td>Variable by kabupaten <span class="data-status uncertain">Uncertain</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>ROI Horizon</th>
                <td>Medium to long</td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Quick Wins</th>
                <td>Upgrading processing (mills, quality control) can unlock value rapidly</td>
              </tr>
            </table>
            
            <h4>Development Opportunities</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Modern processing mills</li>
              <li>Quality standardization</li>
              <li>Industrial starch production</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Maize + Rice Intercropping -->
    <div id="maize-rice" class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header">
        <h2><i class="fas fa-wheat-awn" style="color: #D4A84B;"></i> Maize + Upland Rice Intercropping</h2>
        <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed - Demonstrated Technology</span>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3>Overview</h3>
            <p>Intercropping trials in Maluku with maize and upland rice varieties (Inpago 11, Inpago 12, Rindang 1, Rindang 2) show Land Equivalent Ratio (LER) of 2.11-2.45. <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></p>
            
            <h4>Agro-Ecological Fit</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Targeted at dryland areas with limited irrigation</li>
              <li>Maluku farmers already cultivate maize and upland rice</li>
              <li>Intercropping improves resilience and productivity</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Market Analysis</h4>
            <ul>
              <li><strong>Local:</strong> Fulfills local food needs</li>
              <li><strong>National:</strong> Integrates with national maize feed demand <span class="data-status assumption">Assumption</span></li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3>Performance Data</h3>
            <table class="mb-lg">
              <tr>
                <th>Land Equivalent Ratio</th>
                <td>2.11-2.45 (111-145% productivity gain) <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Maize Potential Yield</th>
                <td>Up to 13.7 t/ha in trials</td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>Farmer Average (Maize)</th>
                <td>~3.20 t/ha - large yield gap <span class="data-status confirmed">Confirmed</span></td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <th>ROI Horizon</th>
                <td>4-5 months; very compatible with Kementan programs</td>
              </tr>
            </table>
            
            <h4>Target Programs</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>DG Tanaman Pangan - seed and Alsintan support</li>
              <li>Quick-win demonstration projects</li>
              <li>Maluku Tengah scaling package</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
