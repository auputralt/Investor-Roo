<section class="section-sm">
  <div class="container">
    <p class="mb-xl">
      This roadmap outlines the phased approach for implementing the dual-region agricultural investment strategy across Lampung Utara and Maluku. The plan progresses from foundation-building activities to full-scale implementation.
    </p>
    
    <!-- Phase 1 -->
    <div class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header" style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #2D5A27 0%, #4A7C47 100%); color: white;">
        <h2 style="color: white; margin: 0;"><i class="fas fa-flag"></i> Phase 1: Foundation</h2>
        <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); margin: 0.5rem 0 0;">Months 1-3</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-database" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Data Collection</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Obtain official peta penggunaan lahan (land-use maps) from Bappeda</li>
              <li>Request peta kesesuaian lahan (land-suitability maps) per kecamatan</li>
              <li>Collect commodity data per kecamatan from Dinas Pertanian/Perkebunan</li>
              <li>Request agro-climate and soil maps from BBSDLP/Balai agroklimat</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h3><i class="fas fa-search" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Ground-Truth Surveys</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Commission rapid surveys in 2-3 focus kecamatan per region</li>
              <li>Validate land status (HGU, APL, kawasan hutan)</li>
              <li>Document current crop patterns and farmer practices</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-users" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Stakeholder Engagement</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Secure endorsement letters from Bupati and Governor</li>
              <li>Convene task force with Sekda/Bappeda leadership</li>
              <li>Engage Dinas Pertanian, Perkebunan, and Penyuluh</li>
              <li>For Maluku: Engage traditional/adat leaders early</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h3><i class="fas fa-clipboard-list" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Farmer Group Formalization</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Identify kelompok tani with administrative readiness</li>
              <li>Ensure NIK, NPWP, land documentation</li>
              <li>Register groups in SIMLUHTAN system</li>
              <li>Prepare RDKK documents</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="info-box info-box-primary mt-lg">
          <div class="info-box-title">
            <i class="fas fa-file-alt"></i>
            Key Deliverables
          </div>
          <div class="info-box-content">
            <ul style="columns: 2; margin-top: 0.5rem;">
              <li>Complete GIS data package for both regions</li>
              <li>Endorsement letters from regional heads</li>
              <li>Task force established with clear TOR</li>
              <li>50+ farmer groups formalized per region</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Phase 2 -->
    <div class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header" style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #D4A84B 0%, #E5C07B 100%); color: #1A1A1A;">
        <h2 style="color: #1A1A1A; margin: 0;"><i class="fas fa-rocket"></i> Phase 2: Short-term Proposals</h2>
        <p style="color: rgba(0,0,0,0.7); margin: 0.5rem 0 0;">Months 3-12</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Lampung Utara</h3>
            <h4>APBN Submissions</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Submit proposals for maize-cassava-peanut clusters</li>
              <li>Request input support (seeds, fertilizers)</li>
              <li>Apply for Alsintan (tractors, harvesters, dryers)</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Quick-Win Projects</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Establish 2-3 demonstration plots (100-500 ha each)</li>
              <li>Farmer training on improved varieties</li>
              <li>Post-harvest handling improvements</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Maluku</h3>
            <h4>APBN Submissions</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Build on Maluku Tengah's 2025 package success</li>
              <li>Submit maize-upland rice intercropping proposals</li>
              <li>Request seed and Alsintan support</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Quick-Win Projects</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Establish intercropping demonstration clusters</li>
              <li>Farmer training on LER-optimized systems</li>
              <li>Small-scale processing equipment</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="info-box info-box-warning mt-lg">
          <div class="info-box-title">
            <i class="fas fa-lightbulb"></i>
            Quick-Win Focus
          </div>
          <div class="info-box-content">
            <p>Phase 2 focuses on <strong>short-cycle commodities</strong> (maize, cassava, peanuts, rice) with 3-5 month ROI. This demonstrates early results to stakeholders and builds momentum for larger investments.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Phase 3 -->
    <div class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header" style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #8B6F47 0%, #A68B5B 100%); color: white;">
        <h2 style="color: white; margin: 0;"><i class="fas fa-seedling"></i> Phase 3: Medium-term Development</h2>
        <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); margin: 0.5rem 0 0;">Year 2-3</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Lampung Utara</h3>
            <h4>Estate Crop Programs</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Submit coffee rejuvenation proposals to DG Perkebunan</li>
              <li>Pepper intensification programs</li>
              <li>Agroforestry pilot projects</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Kawasan Development</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Develop "Kawasan Sentra Komoditas Unggulan" proposal</li>
              <li>Integrate food crops + estate crops</li>
              <li>Position under Food Estate "lokasi prioritas lainnya"</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Maluku</h3>
            <h4>Spice Development</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Nutmeg and clove rejuvenation programs</li>
              <li>Coconut replanting and intensification</li>
              <li>Sago value chain upgrading</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Bioeconomy Infrastructure</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Mini-distillation plants for nutmeg/clove oil</li>
              <li>VCO processing units</li>
              <li>Sago flour mills</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="info-box info-box-primary mt-lg">
          <div class="info-box-title">
            <i class="fas fa-file-signature"></i>
            Key Milestones
          </div>
          <div class="info-box-content">
            <ul style="columns: 2; margin-top: 0.5rem;">
              <li>MoU between kabupaten, investor, and farmer organizations</li>
              <li>Multi-year kawasan proposal submitted</li>
              <li>First estate crop harvests from rejuvenated areas</li>
              <li>Processing infrastructure operational</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Phase 4 -->
    <div class="card mb-xl">
      <div class="card-header" style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #1E5F8A 0%, #2A7AB8 100%); color: white;">
        <h2 style="color: white; margin: 0;"><i class="fas fa-chart-line"></i> Phase 4: Long-term Scale</h2>
        <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); margin: 0.5rem 0 0;">Year 3-5</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card-body">
        <div class="grid grid-2">
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt" style="color: #2D5A27;"></i> Lampung Utara</h3>
            <h4>Food Estate Integration</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Full integration into national Food Estate program</li>
              <li>Scale to 10,000+ ha production area</li>
              <li>Climate-resilient agricultural hub</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Export Orientation</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Coffee and pepper export channels</li>
              <li>Processed cassava products (tapioca, starch)</li>
              <li>Regional feed supply hub</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
          
          <div>
            <h3><i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt" style="color: #1E5F8A;"></i> Maluku</h3>
            <h4>Bioeconomy Hub</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Established spice processing industry</li>
              <li>Nutmeg oil, clove oil export</li>
              <li>VCO and coconut product exports</li>
            </ul>
            
            <h4>Pacific Trade</h4>
            <ul>
              <li>Position as Pacific food and spice supplier</li>
              <li>Eastern Indonesia food estate expansion</li>
              <li>Regional bioeconomy center</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="info-box info-box-primary mt-lg">
          <div class="info-box-title">
            <i class="fas fa-trophy"></i>
            Target Outcomes (Year 5)
          </div>
          <div class="info-box-content">
            <div class="grid grid-2" style="margin-top: 0.5rem;">
              <div>
                <h4>Lampung Utara</h4>
                <ul>
                  <li>10,000+ ha under improved production</li>
                  <li>5,000+ farmer households benefited</li>
                  <li>IDR 50B+ annual agricultural value</li>
                </ul>
              </div>
              <div>
                <h4>Maluku</h4>
                <ul>
                  <li>5,000+ ha spice production area</li>
                  <li>3,000+ farmer households benefited</li>
                  <li>Processing industry established</li>
                </ul>
              </div>
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

          <ul style="margin-top: 0.5rem;">
            <li><strong>Lampung Utara:</strong> Mainland food and estate-crop hub</li>
            <li><strong>Maluku:</strong> Archipelagic bioeconomy and spice hub</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div class="info-box info-box-primary">
        <div class="info-box-title">
          <i class="fas fa-layer-group"></i>

        </div>

          <p>Combine multiple support mechanisms:</p>
          <ul style="margin-top: 0.5rem;">
            <li>Immediate APBN support (seeds, inputs, Alsintan)</li>
            <li>Medium-term estate crop rejuvenation</li>
            <li>Long-term food-estate integration</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

        <ol style="margin-top: 0.5rem;">
          <li><strong>Political commitment:</strong> Secure and maintain endorsement from Bupati and Governor</li>
          <li><strong>Data quality:</strong> Invest in accurate land and commodity data</li>
          <li><strong>Farmer organization:</strong> Strong kelompok tani are essential for program access</li>
